SOLO LEVELING

Solo Leveling follows Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest Hunter in a world where people fight monsters in dungeon raids. After a near-fatal dungeon encounter, he's chosen by a mysterious “System” that lets him level up infinitely. As he grows stronger, he uncovers dark secrets behind the dungeons and his newfound power. Rising from an E-rank weakling to the world's most powerful Hunter, Jin-Woo battles deadly foes and challenges humanity's limits. With epic fights, thrilling progression, and an overpowered protagonist, Solo Leveling is an action-packed adventure perfect for fans of fantasy, leveling systems, and high-stakes battles.
