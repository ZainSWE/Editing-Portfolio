JUJUTSU KAISEN

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high schooler who gains supernatural powers after swallowing a cursed object—Ryomen Sukuna's finger. Now the vessel of the King of Curses, he's recruited by Satoru Gojo into Tokyo Jujutsu High to learn Jujutsu Sorcery and battle deadly Curses. Alongside Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, Yuji faces powerful enemies, including rogue sorcerers and monstrous beings. As he consumes more of Sukuna's fingers, he struggles with his growing power and moral dilemmas. With breathtaking action, emotional depth, and supernatural horror, Jujutsu Kaisen is a must-watch for fans of dark fantasy and intense battle anime.
