KUNG FU PANDA

Bullet Train follows Ladybug, a reluctant assassin tasked with retrieving a briefcase on a Japanese bullet train. What should be a simple job turns into chaos when he encounters other assassins—each with their own motives. As the train speeds forward, betrayals, deadly fights, and shocking twists unfold. Ladybug, trying to avoid unnecessary violence, gets caught in a web of fate connecting all passengers. Packed with intense action, sharp humor, and stylish fight sequences, Bullet Train, directed by David Leitch, delivers a wild, fast-paced ride that blends comedy and thriller elements into a highly entertaining and unpredictable adventure.
